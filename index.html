Location

Kaiteriteri — paradise found

Kaiteriteri is one of New Zealand's most beloved coastal villages, nestled at the gateway to Abel Tasman National Park. Bike & Beach puts you right in the heart of it all.

5 min flat walk to the beach Kaiteriteri's famous golden sands — stroll down any time of day
